Game Mode: Major Areas
	-3rd Person Action
	-object interaction
	-mob interaction

Game Mode: Combat Zone
	-isometric on hex board
	-occupents of combat zone roll initiative/awareness
	-action sequence:
		-move, attack/action, end
		-attack/action, move, end
		-attack/action, 1/2 move, attack/action,end
	-mode last till one opponent remains in combat zone

Game Mode: Road Travel/Fast Travel
	-Road Travel:
		-done on 2d map point to point
		-# types of points
			-Major Point
			-Minor Point
			-Crossroad
		-variable random encounters
	-Fast Travel
		-done on 2d map by list
		-pathfind most direct route
		-stiil will have random encounters
